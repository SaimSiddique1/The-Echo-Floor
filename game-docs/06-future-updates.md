# 06 — Future Updates

## The Architect (Post-Launch Role)

### Concept
One player per round is secretly shown the anomaly before the floor opens. They play alongside everyone else with **perfect knowledge**. Their goal is to subtly guide the group to the correct object.

### Mechanics
- Architect is selected randomly before the floor loads
- Architect sees a 3-second overlay: *"You are the Architect. The anomaly is: [object name]"*
- Architect searches and votes normally
- If the group finds the anomaly: Architect gets **bonus points**
- If the group fails: Architect gets **nothing** (no penalty — they just didn't guide well enough)
- If the Architect reports: they are visibly shown as the reporter — creates social tension
- If the Architect votes YES on a wrong object: other players might catch on

### Why It Works
- Adds a hidden-layer social dynamic without requiring a "vote out" mechanic
- Creates replayability — same anomaly feels different when you know vs. when you don't
- Architects develop their own guiding strategies (pointing, standing near, chat hints)
- New players get a free learning round as Architect

### Future Variations
- **The Saboteur**: Opposite of Architect — knows the anomaly and tries to mislead (hard mode)
- **The Observer**: Spectator who can see all player positions and anomalies — for streaming/competitive modes

- Skins are purely visual — zero gameplay advantage
